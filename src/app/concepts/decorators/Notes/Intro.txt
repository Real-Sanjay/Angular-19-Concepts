 What is a Decorator in Angular?
In Angular, decorators are special functions prefixed with @ that add metadata to classes, methods, properties, or parameters. They help Angular understand how to process and instantiate various elements in your app.

@Pipe	    Tells Angular it's a custom pipe
@NgModule	Tells Angular it's a module (container of components, directives, services, etc.)
