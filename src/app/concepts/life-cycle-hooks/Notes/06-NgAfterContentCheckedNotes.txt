What is ngAfterContentChecked() in Angular?
ngAfterContentChecked is a lifecycle hook in Angular that's triggered every time Angular checks the content projected into a component (via <ng-content>). This check happens after each change detection cycle.

Why is this important?
If you're using content projection (<ng-content>) and you want to perform logic when that content is re-checked, such as:
Logging or debugging
Validating inputs
Tracking changes in content
This hook helps you monitor or act every time that projected content is re-evaluated.
